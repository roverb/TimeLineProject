The Adventure TimeLine

Including All Important Events In, Before and After The Show.

  • 2047

    This event destroyed pretty much all life in The Land of OOO. A couple of humans escaped the nuclear explosion and fled to the sewer and other underground habits. Susan Strong and her people might be a couple of these survivors. The creatures and humans that didn't get away were killed by the blast, radiation, the Lich or were mutated in new creatures like the candy people. Most of the other inhabitant of The Land of OOO come from different dimensions. Marcaline is born a couple of years before the disaster and meets Simon Petrikov a couple of hours after the destruction, as there is still smoke and fire in the air. She probably didn't die because of her being a Vampire already or having immortality from her father.

  • +/- 2047

    This war probably occured several years after the GMW (Great Mushroom War). It has been stated that the Rainicorns and Dogs fought over territory in the Crystal Dimension. This could be doubted because on the drawing made by Jake smooth hills can be seen. These cannot be found in the Crystal Dimension. The Adventure Time wiki states that the Ranicorns are from The Candy Kingdom or The Crystal Dimension.
